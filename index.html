About Me

Hello! I am Zoe Stephens, a third-year student-athlete at Wellesley College majoring in Cognitive Science with a concentration in Computer Science. As a student-athlete on the varsity soccer team, I thrive in challenging situations, and am constantly pushing myself and my teammates to improve.

My broad interest in the sciences allows me to bring a multidisciplinary viewpoint to my projects and better understand the interactions between different fields. I am currently working in a Biology lab researching behavioral patterns of Southern Giant Hornets, and I am also involved with writing a sociolinguistics proceedings paper about text-formatting.

I believe strongly in passion and curiosity as the driving factors of innovation, and I am always looking for ways to be more involved in the STEM research community. Reach out to zoestephe@gmail.com to connect!
