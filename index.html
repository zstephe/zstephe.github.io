About Me

Hello! I am Zoe Stephens, a third-year student-athlete at Wellesley College majoring in Cognitive Science with a concentration in Computer Science. When I'm not studying, I enjoy spending time baking, reading, and lifting weights.

I am currently working in a Biology lab researching behavioral patterns of Southern Giant Hornets. I am also involved with writing a sociolinguistics proceedings paper about text-formatting, and I hope to continue to build off of this research in the future. I am interested in a wide range of subject matter—including biology, computer science, and linguistics—as well as how these fields interact.

I believe strongly in passion and curiosity as the driving factors of innovation, and I am always looking for ways to be more involved in the STEM research community. Reach out to zoestephe@gmail.com to connect!
